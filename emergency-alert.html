Self Defense

Hammer Strike

Using your car keys is one of the easiest ways to defend yourself. Don’t use your fingernails; instead, hold your key ring in a tight fist, like holding a hammer, with keys extending from the side of your hand. Thrust downward toward your target.

Groin Kick

If someone is coming at you from the front, a groin kick may deliver enough force to paralyze your attacker, making your escape possible. Stabilize yourself and drive your knee upward, kicking forcefully toward the attacker’s groin area.

Heel Palm Strike

This move can cause damage to the nose or throat. Aim for the attacker’s nose or underneath their chin, jabbing upward. Recoil your strike to help thrust the attacker’s head back, allowing you to escape.

Elbow Strike

If your attacker is in close range, bend your arm at the elbow and strike into their neck, jawline, chin, or temple. This may cause them to loosen their grip, allowing you to run.

Escape from Bear Hug

If the attacker is coming from behind, bend forward from the waist to shift your weight forward. Use elbows to strike at the attacker’s face and create space to escape.

Escape with Hands Trapped

If your arms are trapped, stop the attacker from going higher into a headlock. Shift your hips to one side for an opening to strike the groin and turn into the wrap to escape.

Escape from Side Headlock

Turn into the attacker’s side to avoid choking. Strike the groin until you have enough mobility to disengage and escape.
